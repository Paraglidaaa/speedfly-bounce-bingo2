Welcome to Speedfly Bounce Bingo

Do you know a pilot who is constantly pushing their limits a bit too far? Is just plain incompetent? Is starting to get complacent? Bounce Bingo is an anonymous but hopefully impactful way of letting them know what you think and maybe making that person reconsider their approach. If enough people name the same person, maybe they'll sit up and take notice. Have you seen yourself on the list? Maybe its time to dial it back, or further your education.

Please be respectful and constructive when participating.
